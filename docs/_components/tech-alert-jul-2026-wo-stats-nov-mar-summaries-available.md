Digital Earth Australia has released the latest Water Observations Statistics (Nov–Mar) seasonal summaries for 2025–26.  

Derived from Landsat satellite data, these summaries capture where surface water was observed during the November–March period, providing a consistent national view of seasonal water patterns. 

The updated summaries enable users to better understand seasonal variability in rivers, wetlands and floodplains. By highlighting areas of persistent water and short-term change, the dataset supports analysis of flooding, drought and environmental response to climate variability. 

These seasonal products complement DEA’s long-term water datasets, offering additional context for research, planning and decision-making. 

You are now able to access the data via [DEA Maps](https://maps.dea.ga.gov.au/#share=s-bQPsM0RuRszMOBTEUM3XdD4rk86 ) with further information available on the [DEA Knowledge Hub](/data/product/dea-water-observations-statistics-landsat/ ).
